Welcome to ZOC CUT

ZAOGA ON CAMPUS CHINHOYI UNIVERSITY OF TECHNOLOGY ZOC is under the the University Graduate Fellowship ( UGF) which was founded in the year 1991 with our Father, Apostle E H Guti. I would be remise if l don't talk about the first ZOC Chairman who is the Late Apostle Atoria Gudyanga. ZOC CUT is a home away from home It is a place where leaders are made, families are found and identities are made clearer It's at ZOC CUT where you realise your true identity in Christ The purpose of ZOC is for a graduate to preach to another graduate Soul winning is our mandate, it's the number one thing We usually have inreaches, where students preach on campus, moving from one hostel to another, room by room Outreaches are carried out from various areas within the Vicinity of Mashonaland West Province.

A team of Young, vibrant Evangelists goes in their numbers to witness the Good news and Win souls unto the Kingdom of God, after all He who wins souls is wise Business people are made in ZOC CUT, Entrepreneurs, Worshippers, rhythmic and vibrant drummers and Intercessors, just to mention a few. At ZOC we:

  • Network,
  • Fellowship,
  • Make family and friends.

ZOC CUT, ONE PLACE TO BE!!
